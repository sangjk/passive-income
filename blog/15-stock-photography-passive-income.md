## How to Build Your Stock Portfolio

### Phase 1: The First 100 Photos (1–2 months)

Your goal: get 100 high-quality photos approved and listed.

**Shoot everything:**
- Everyday objects around your home (coffee cup, desk setup, plants)
- Family and friends in natural settings (with model releases)
- Your city — landmarks, streets, parks
- Seasons and weather in your area
- Your hobbies and activities

**Tips for fast approval:**
- Study what's already selling on each platform
- Focus on technically excellent photos (sharp, well-exposed)
- Avoid logos, brands, and copyrighted elements
- Get model releases signed for any recognizable people
- Submit photos in batches of 10–20

### Phase 2: Data-Driven Shooting (3–6 months)

After 3 months, analyze what's selling:

1. Check your top 10 selling photos — what category, style, subject?
2. Shoot more in your winning categories
3. Look for seasonal gaps (your Christmas photos should be uploaded by September)
4. Check platform trend reports for what buyers are searching for

### Phase 3: Scaling (6+ months)

- **Batch shooting** — Dedicate one day per week to shooting
- **Seasonal planning** — Shoot holidays 3–4 months ahead
- **Video content** — Expand to stock video (higher royalties)
- **Outsource editing** — Pay editors $0.25–$1 per photo to process your RAW files
- **Build keyword templates** — Save time on metadata

## Keywords and Metadata — The Hidden Skill

Your photo can be the best in the world, but if nobody finds it, it earns nothing. Metadata makes your photos discoverable.

### Title Formula

```
[Main Subject] + [Action/Context] + [Setting] + [Key Descriptors]
```

- ❌ "IMG_4829.jpg"
- ✅ "Young woman working on laptop at modern coffee shop with plants"

### Keyword Strategy

Include 25–50 keywords per photo covering:

1. **Who/what** — "woman, adult, millennial, entrepreneur"
2. **Action** — "working, typing, writing, focused"
3. **Setting** — "coffee shop, cafe, interior, modern"
4. **Objects** — "laptop, coffee cup, notebook, plants"
5. **Concepts** — "remote work, productivity, technology, lifestyle"
6. **Emotions** — "focused, calm, relaxed, confident"
7. **Composition** — "horizontal, copy space, natural light, candid"
