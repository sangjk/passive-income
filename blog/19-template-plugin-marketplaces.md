## How to Find Winning Template Ideas

### Research Method

1. **Browse marketplaces** — Sort by "Best Sellers" on ThemeForest, Creative Market, Gumroad
2. **Note gaps** — What niche is underserved? What category has few options?
3. **Check reviews** — Read complaints on existing products. Build what customers are asking for.
4. **Look at velocity** — Sort by "New & Trending" to spot emerging categories
5. **Price analysis** — Check the $20–$80 range for templates. Premium is $80–$200.

### The "Small Niche" Strategy

Instead of "business templates" (too broad), try:
- "Invoice templates for freelance photographers"
- "Social media templates for real estate agents"
- "Lesson plan templates for music teachers"

Specificity reduces competition and increases perceived value.

## Launch and Marketing

### Marketplace Launch (Built-In Traffic)

1. Create your product and screenshots
2. Write your listing (title, description, tags — all SEO-optimized)
3. Submit for review (1–7 days for most marketplaces)
4. First 30 days: lower price to get initial sales and reviews
5. Respond to reviews and update based on feedback

### External Marketing

| Channel | Strategy |
|---------|----------|
| **YouTube** | Template walkthrough and setup tutorial |
| **Twitter/X** | Share design process, launch announcement |
| **Reddit** | Share in niche subreddits (follow rules) |
| **Product Hunt** | Launch there for spike of attention |
| **Email list** | Announce new templates to past buyers |
| **SEO content** | Blog posts about problems your template solves |
