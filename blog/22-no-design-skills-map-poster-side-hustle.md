## The "100-Product Rule"

Here's the single most important thing to understand about this business:

**Your first 10 products are a test. Products 11–100 are where the money starts.**

Why? Three reasons:

1. **Etsy SEO takes time** — New listings need 2–4 weeks to index and rank
2. **The numbers favor volume** — 100 products at 1% conversion rate on 100 views each = 100 sales/month. 10 products = 10 sales/month.
3. **Data reveals winners** — With 100 products, you'll see which cities and styles sell best. Double down on winners.

Poster Maps Pro makes the 100-product goal achievable in a weekend — not months.
