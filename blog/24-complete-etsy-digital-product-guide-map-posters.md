## Part 3: Etsy SEO — The Engine of Passive Sales

### How Etsy Search Works

Etsy's algorithm considers:

1. **Relevance** — How well your listing matches the search query (title, tags, description)
2. **Listing quality** — Click-through rate, favorites, conversion rate
3. **Recency** — New listings get a temporary boost (use this!)
4. **Customer experience** — Reviews, response time, shipping speed
5. **Shop quality** — Overall shop performance and policies

### Title Optimization

**Formula:** [Primary Keyword] | [Style] [City] [Product Type] | [Use Case] [Room]

```
Bad: "Paris map print"

Good: "Paris City Map Poster | Minimalist France Wall Art | 
Black & White Digital Download | French Home Decor Print"
```

**Title checklist:**
- [ ] Primary keyword in first 40 characters
- [ ] City name + "map" + "poster" or "print" or "wall art"
- [ ] Style descriptor (minimalist, vintage, modern, etc.)
- [ ] Use case (home decor, gift, office wall art)
- [ ] 120–140 characters total (use the space)

### Tag Strategy (13 Tags)

Etsy gives you 13 tag slots. Use all of them. Every single one.

**Tag formula — cover these categories:**

| Category | Example Tags |
|----------|-------------|
| **Product type** | "city map print," "map poster," "wall art" |
| **City/place** | "paris print," "france decor," "europe art" |
| **Style** | "minimalist poster," "black white art," "line art print" |
| **Use case** | "home decor," "gift for traveler," "apartment art" |
| **Room** | "living room decor," "bedroom wall art," "office print" |
| **Format** | "digital download," "printable art," "instant download" |
| **Audience** | "gift for him," "housewarming gift," "travel lover gift" |

### Description Best Practices

Your description serves two purposes: convincing the buyer and feeding Etsy's algorithm.

**Structure:**

1. **Opening hook** (2 sentences) — Why this product, emotional connection
2. **What's included** — File formats, sizes, ratios
3. **How it works** — Purchase → download → print → enjoy
4. **Perfect for** — Use cases (gift, home office, etc.)
5. **Shop promise** — Satisfaction guarantee, quality
6. **FAQ** — Anticipate and answer buyer questions

4. **Sale events** — Run 20% off sales during Etsy's site-wide events and holidays.

## Part 5: Mockups — The Silent Salesperson

### Why Mockups Matter

Your product thumbnail is the #1 factor in click-through rate. A great map poster with a bad mockup = zero sales.

### Mockup Strategy

Show your map poster in context:

1. **Hero image:** Map in a modern frame on a wall in a styled room
2. **Close-up:** Detail shot showing the map quality and street detail
3. **Lifestyle:** Map in a bedroom, living room, or office setting
4. **Multiple frames:** Same map in different frame styles (black, white, wood)
5. **Size comparison:** Show relative sizes (small / medium / large on wall)

### Mockup Tools

| Tool | Best For | Cost |
|------|----------|------|
| **Placeit** | Frame mockups, lifestyle scenes | $15/mo |
| **Canva** | Simple frame overlays, collages | Free / $13/mo |
| **Smartmockups** | Professional product mockups | Free / $9/mo |
| **Photoshop** | Advanced customization | $23/mo |
