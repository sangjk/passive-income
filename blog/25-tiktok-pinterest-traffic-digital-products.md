## Part 1: TikTok Strategy for Map Poster Sellers

### Why TikTok Works for Digital Products

"TikTok made me buy it" is a cultural phenomenon. The platform drives purchase intent like nothing else. And digital products — especially aesthetic, visual ones like map posters — are perfectly suited to short-form video.

### Content Pillars for Map Poster Content

**Pillar 1: Creation Process (ASMR / Satisfying)**

Show the Poster Maps Pro creation workflow in 15–30 seconds:

- City name appears → style selector → map generates → final poster reveal
- Add satisfying background music
- Text overlay: "Making a Tokyo map poster in 30 seconds 🗺️"

**Why it works:** The transformation is visually satisfying. People watch creation content even when they have no intent to buy — which means the algorithm pushes it to more people, some of whom WILL buy.

**Pillar 2: Product Showcase (Aesthetic)**

Showcase the final product in beautiful settings:

- Styled room with frame mockup
- Multiple cities in a gallery wall arrangement
- Before/after: blank wall → wall with map poster
- "3 ways to style a city map poster in your apartment"

**Pillar 3: Educational / Side Hustle**

Teach others how you're making money:

- "How I made $500 this month selling digital products"
- "No design skills? Here's my side hustle secret"
- "I create these in 60 seconds and sell them on Etsy"
- "Day 1 of my Etsy digital product challenge"

**Why it works:** Side hustle content is massively popular. When you share your method, people want to replicate your success. Some will buy your products to support you. Some will become customers because they don't want to do it themselves.

**Pillar 4: Emotional / Story-Driven**

Connect products to emotion:

- "A map of the city where we met 💍"
- "POV: You're moving away from your hometown"
- "Gift idea for the person who misses their city"
- "The perfect housewarming gift nobody thinks of"

| Day | Content Type | Time to Create |
|-----|-------------|---------------|
| **Monday** | Creation process (new city) | 5 min |
| **Tuesday** | Product showcase (styled mockup) | 10 min |

**Total weekly time: ~50 minutes for 7 pieces of content**

### TikTok Optimization Checklist

- [ ] Video length: 15–30 seconds (optimal for reach)
- [ ] Hook in first 1–2 seconds (text on screen or verbal)
- [ ] Trending sound (check TikTok trends daily)
- [ ] Caption: 2–3 lines + 3–5 relevant hashtags
- [ ] Link in bio: Etsy shop or Gumroad
- [ ] Reply to comments: boosts engagement signals
- [ ] Post time: when your audience is active (typically 7–9 PM local)

## Part 2: Pinterest Strategy for Map Poster Sellers

### Why Pinterest Is Your Secret Weapon

Pinterest is not social media — it's a visual search engine. Users come to Pinterest with intent: planning purchases, saving ideas, and making buying decisions. This is fundamentally different from TikTok's entertainment-first model.

For Etsy sellers, Pinterest is often the #1 external traffic source — sometimes driving more visits than Etsy search itself.

### Pinterest Content Strategy for Map Posters

**Board Structure**

| Board Name | Content | SEO Keywords in Board Description |
|-----------|---------|----------------------------------|
| City Map Wall Art | Your products (all cities, all styles) | "city map poster, map wall art, city print" |
| Home Office Decor Ideas | Map posters in office settings | "home office wall art, WFH decor" |
| Apartment Aesthetic | Lifestyle mockups | "apartment wall decor, first apartment" |
| Gift Ideas for Travelers | Gift-oriented pins | "gift for traveler, unique gift ideas" |
| Minimalist Home Decor | Minimalist style maps | "minimalist wall art, modern home decor" |
| Digital Products Side Hustle | Business/income content | "Etsy digital products, passive income" |

### Pin Creation Strategy

For every Poster Maps Pro product you create, make 3–5 pins:

1. **The Product Pin** — Clean product image + city name + "Digital Download"
2. **The Lifestyle Pin** — Product in a styled room mockup
3. **The Comparison Pin** — "3 Styles for [City]" (minimal vs vintage vs modern)
4. **The Bundle Pin** — "5 European Cities Map Set"
5. **The Text Overlay Pin** — "Perfect Gift for [Audience]" on top of product

### Pinterest SEO (The Real Magic)

Pinterest is a search engine. Optimize for it:

**Pin Title Formula:**

```
[City Name] [Style] Map Poster | [Use Case] | Digital Download Wall Art
```

**Pin Description Formula:**

```
[Keyword-rich first sentence about the product.]
[What makes it special — style, quality, uniqueness.]
[Perfect for — who is this for, what room, what occasion.]
[Call to action — click to see more / shop now.]
[5–8 relevant hashtags]

Example:
Minimalist New York City map poster — perfect for modern home office 
decor. High-resolution digital download, print at home or at your local 
print shop. Makes a thoughtful gift for NYC lovers. Click to see all 
available styles in my Etsy shop.

#citymapposter #nycwallart #digitaldownload #homedecor #mapdesign
```

### Pinterest Scheduling

| Frequency | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| **Daily** | Pin 5–10 images (your products + others' relevant content) |
| **Weekly** | Create 3–5 new pin designs from your latest products |
| **Monthly** | Review analytics — which pins are driving clicks? Create similar. |

**Tool:** Use Tailwind ($15/mo) to schedule a month of pins in one sitting. This makes Pinterest truly passive.
