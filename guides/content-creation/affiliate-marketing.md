# 🛒 Affiliate Marketing — Side Hustle Guide

## Overview

Recommend products you trust and earn commissions on sales. Works on any platform — blog, social media, YouTube, email. One of the most flexible side hustles.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Difficulty** | ⭐⭐ (Easy–Medium) |
| **Time to First $** | 1–3 months |
| **Earning Potential** | $500–$10,000+/month |
| **Upfront Investment** | $0–$50/month |
| **Best Platforms** | Blog, YouTube, Email, Social Media |

---

## Step 1: Understand Affiliate Marketing

### How It Works

```
You create content → Reader clicks your link → They buy → You earn commission
```

### Commission Models

| Model | Description | Typical Rate |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| **Revenue share** | % of sale price | 5–50% |
| **Flat fee** | Fixed amount per sale | $1–$200 |
| **Recurring** | % of ongoing subscription | 20–40% monthly |
| **Per lead** | Fixed amount per sign-up | $1–$50 |
| **Per click** | Paid per click (rare) | $0.01–$0.50 |

### Program Selection Criteria

1. **Relevance** — Does this product serve your audience?
2. **Commission rate** — Is it worth the effort to promote?
3. **Cookie duration** — How long do you get credit? (30–90 days typical)
4. **Product quality** — Would you actually recommend this?
5. **Conversion rate** — Does the merchant's site convert well?

## Step 3: Build Your Platform

### Platform Options

| Platform | Setup Time | Best Affiliate Strategy |
|----------|-----------|----------------------|
| **Blog** | 2–4 weeks | SEO-driven product reviews |
| **YouTube** | 2–4 weeks | Video reviews & tutorials |
| **Email list** | 4–8 weeks | Curated recommendations |
| **Social media** | 1 day | Short-form product showcases |
| **Podcast** | 4–8 weeks | Audio reviews & mentions |

### Content Types That Convert

1. **Product reviews** — "Is [Product] Worth It? Honest Review"
2. **Comparisons** — "[Product A] vs [Product B]: Which Should You Buy?"
3. **Tutorials** — "How to Use [Product] to [Achieve Result]"
4. **Roundups** — "Best [Number] [Products] for [Use Case]"
5. **Case studies** — "How I Used [Product] to [Achieve Result]"
6. **Alternatives** — "7 [Product] Alternatives That Are Cheaper"
7. **Gift guides** — "Best Gifts for [Person Type] in 2026"

### SEO Strategy for Affiliate Content

1. **Target buyer-intent keywords** — "best [product]," "[product] review," "[product] vs [product]"
2. **Create comparison tables** — Readers love side-by-side comparisons
3. **Use pros/cons lists** — Honesty builds trust
4. **Include real photos** — Your own product images > stock photos
5. **Update regularly** — Keep prices and features current

## Step 5: Optimize & Scale

### Conversion Optimization

| Tactic | Impact | Effort |
|--------|--------|--------|
| **Add comparison tables** | High | Low |
| **Use "Best for" labels** | Medium | Low |
| **Include personal experience** | High | Medium |
| **Add call-to-action buttons** | Medium | Low |
| **Show real results/screenshots** | High | Medium |
| **Create urgency** | Medium | Low |
| **Build email sequences** | Very High | High |

### Scaling Strategies

1. **Multiply content** — More reviews = more traffic = more sales
2. **Expand to new products** — Add related products in your niche
3. **Build email funnels** — Automated sequences that sell on autopilot
4. **Negotiate higher rates** — Once you drive volume, ask for better commissions
5. **Create bonus offers** — "Buy through my link and get [bonus]"

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

1. **Promoting too many products** — Focus on 5–10 quality products
2. **Not disclosing affiliate links** — Legal requirement in most countries
3. **Recommending bad products** — Short-term gain, long-term trust loss
4. **Only promoting, never providing value** — 80% helpful content, 20% promotion
5. **Ignoring email lists** — Your most valuable affiliate asset
