# ✍️ Blog / Newsletter — Side Hustle Guide

## Overview

Write in-depth articles in a specific niche. Monetize through ads, affiliate marketing, sponsorships, and subscriptions. Long-term compounding asset.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Difficulty** | ⭐⭐ (Easy–Medium) |
| **Time to First $** | 2–6 months |
| **Earning Potential** | $500–$10,000+/month |
| **Upfront Investment** | $0–$20/month (hosting) |
| **Best Platforms** | Substack, Ghost, WordPress, Medium |

---

## Step 1: Choose Your Platform

### Platform Comparison

| Platform | Cost | Revenue Share | Best For |
|----------|------|--------------|----------|
| **Substack** | Free | 10% of paid subs | Newsletter-first, paid subscriptions |
| **Ghost** | $9–$199/mo | 0% (you keep all) | Full control, professional |
| **WordPress** | $3–$30/mo | 0% | Full customization, SEO |
| **Medium** | Free | Partner program | Building audience, no setup |
| **Beehiiv** | Free–$99/mo | 0% | Newsletter growth, ad network |

### Recommendation

- **Starting out:** Substack or Beehiiv (free, easy setup)
- **Growing:** Ghost or WordPress (more control, better SEO)
- **Testing ideas:** Medium (zero setup, built-in audience)

### Niche Selection Criteria

1. **You have genuine interest** — You'll write 50+ articles about this
2. **People search for it** — Use Google Trends, Ahrefs free tools
3. **It has monetization paths** — Products, services, or affiliates exist
4. **You have unique insight** — Personal experience > generic advice

## Step 3: Create Content That Ranks

### SEO-Driven Content Strategy

1. **Keyword research** — Find what people search for
2. **Create pillar content** — 3–5 comprehensive guides (2,000+ words)
3. **Support with cluster posts** — 10–20 shorter articles linking to pillars
4. **Update regularly** — Refresh top posts every 3–6 months

### Article Template

```markdown
# [Keyword-Rich Title]

## Introduction (hook + what you'll learn)

## [Section 1: The Problem/Context]

## [Section 2: The Solution/Method]

## [Section 3: Step-by-Step Guide]

## [Section 4: Examples/Case Studies]

## [Section 5: Common Mistakes to Avoid]

## Summary / Key Takeaways

## Call to Action
```

### Writing Best Practices

- **Headlines matter** — Use numbers, specificity, and curiosity
- **First paragraph** — Hook the reader in 2 sentences
- **Use headers** — Break content into scannable sections
- **Add images** — Every 300–500 words
- **Internal linking** — Link to 2–3 of your other posts
- **Meta description** — 150–160 characters, includes keyword

## Step 4: Grow Your Audience

### Growth Channels

| Channel | Time Investment | Subscriber Quality |
|---------|----------------|-------------------|
| **SEO** | High upfront, passive later | Very High |
| **Social media** | Medium ongoing | Medium |
| **Cross-promotions** | Low | High |
| **Guest posting** | Medium | High |
| **Email swaps** | Low | Medium–High |
| **Paid ads** | Low time, costs money | Varies |

### Newsletter Growth Tactics

1. **Lead magnet** — Free resource in exchange for email (checklist, template, mini-course)
2. **Welcome sequence** — 3–5 email onboarding series
3. **Consistent schedule** — Weekly or bi-weekly, same day/time
4. **Shareable content** — Write posts people want to forward
5. **Community building** — Comments section, Discord, or Slack group

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

1. **Writing for yourself, not your audience** — Solve reader problems
2. **Inconsistent publishing** — One post/week minimum
3. **Ignoring SEO** — Organic search is the best free traffic source
4. **Monetizing too early** — Build trust before selling
5. **Not building an email list** — You don't own social media followers
