## Step 3: Grow Your Audience

### Growth Tactics

1. **Post consistently** — Minimum 4–5 times per week
2. **Use trending keywords** — Search the platform for what's hot
3. **Engage for 30 min/day** — Comment on posts in your niche
4. **Collaborate** — Find accounts at your level, do joint posts
5. **Optimize posting times** — Post when your audience is active

### Xiaohongshu-Specific Tips

- Use Chinese keywords in titles and descriptions
- Include 6–9 images per post (more = more exposure)
- Add location tags for local discovery
- Use relevant hashtags (3–5 per post)
- Write detailed, helpful descriptions (300+ words)

### Instagram-Specific Tips

- Use Reels for maximum reach (algorithm prioritizes video)
- Create shareable carousel posts
- Use 20–30 relevant hashtags
- Engage within 30 minutes of posting
- Build relationships through DMs and stories

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

1. **Copying without adapting** — Direct translations feel inauthentic
2. **Inconsistent posting** — The algorithm rewards consistency
3. **Buying followers** — Fake followers = no engagement = no money
4. **Ignoring analytics** — Track what works and double down
5. **Only selling, never providing value** — 80% value, 20% promotion
