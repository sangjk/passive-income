## Step 3: Create Your First 10 Videos

### Video Ideas Generator

For any niche, create these 10 video types:

1. **"What is [topic]?"** — Beginner introduction
2. **"How to [achieve result]"** — Step-by-step tutorial
3. **"[Number] mistakes to avoid with [topic]"** — List format
4. **"[Tool/Product] review"** — Honest review
5. **"[Tool A] vs [Tool B]"** — Comparison
6. **"My [time period] journey with [topic]"** — Personal story
7. **"Best [number] [tools/methods] for [topic]"** — Roundup
8. **"Why [common belief] is wrong"** — Contrarian take
9. **"Complete guide to [topic]"** — Comprehensive tutorial
10. **"Q&A: Answering your [topic] questions"** — Community engagement |

### Video Production Workflow

1. **Research** — Find top-ranking videos, identify gaps (30 min)
2. **Script/Outline** — Write bullet points or full script (1 hour)
3. **Record** — Film or screen record (1–2 hours)
4. **Edit** — Cut, add graphics, music, captions (2–4 hours)
5. **Thumbnail** — Create click-worthy thumbnail (20 min)
6. **Upload & Optimize** — Title, description, tags, cards (20 min)

## Step 4: Grow Your Channel

### YouTube Algorithm Factors

| Factor | Weight | How to Optimize |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| **Click-through rate (CTR)** | Very High | Compelling thumbnails + titles |
| **Average view duration** | Very High | Strong hooks, engaging content |
| **Engagement** | High | Ask for likes, comments, subscribes |
| **Upload frequency** | Medium | Consistent schedule (1–2/week) |
| **Session time** | Medium | Create playlists, end screens |

### Thumbnail Best Practices

1. **3 elements max** — Face + text + object
2. **Readable at small size** — Large, bold text (3–5 words)
3. **High contrast colors** — Stand out in the feed
4. **Emotional faces** — Surprise, confusion, excitement
5. **Consistent style** — Build brand recognition

### Title Formulas

- "How I [achieved result] in [timeframe]"
- "[Number] [things] You're Doing Wrong With [topic]"
- "I Tried [method] for [timeframe] — Here's What Happened"
- "The Complete Guide to [topic] (Beginner to Advanced)"
- "[Tool/Method] vs [Tool/Method]: Which is Better in 2026?"

## Step 5: Monetize

### YouTube Monetization Requirements

- **1,000 subscribers** + **4,000 watch hours** (past 12 months), OR
- **1,000 subscribers** + **10 million Shorts views** (past 90 days)

### Revenue Streams

| Method | Requirements | Monthly Income |
|--------|-------------|---------------|
| **AdSense** | Monetization threshold | $2–$15 per 1,000 views |

| **Affiliate links** | Any | $50–$2,000/month |
| **Channel memberships** | 1K+ subscribers | $5–$50/member |
| **Merchandise** | 10K+ subscribers | Varies |
| **Digital products** | Any | $100–$5,000/month |

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

1. **Quitting too early** — Most channels take 6–12 months to gain traction
2. **Ignoring thumbnails** — Great video + bad thumbnail = no views
3. **Inconsistent upload schedule** — Algorithm and viewers love consistency
4. **Not analyzing analytics** — Study what works and replicate it
5. **Trying to go viral** — Build a library of useful content instead
