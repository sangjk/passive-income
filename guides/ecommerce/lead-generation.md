## Step 1: Understand Lead Generation

### What Is a Lead?

A lead = contact information of a potential customer for a business.

### Lead Types & Pricing

| Lead Type | Data Included | Price per Lead |
|-----------|--------------|---------------|
| **Basic contact** | Name, email, company | $0.50–$5 |
| **Enriched contact** | Name, email, phone, title, company size | $2–$15 |
| **Qualified lead** | Enriched + verified interest/intent | $10–$50 |
| **Appointment set** | Qualified + meeting scheduled | $50–$200 |

### Business Model Options

| Model | Description | Revenue |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **List selling** | Sell a batch of leads | $100–$500 per list |
| **Subscription** | Monthly lead delivery | $200–$2,000/mo per client |
| **Pay-per-lead** | Charge per qualified lead | $5–$50 per lead |
| **Appointment setting** | Book meetings for clients | $50–$200 per appointment |
| **Retainer** | Monthly fee for ongoing service | $1,000–$5,000/mo |

### Niche Selection Criteria

1. **High customer lifetime value** — Businesses that earn $5K+ per client will pay well for leads
2. **Clear target persona** — You can identify and find the decision-maker
3. **Data availability** — Contact info is accessible (LinkedIn, directories)
4. **Your knowledge** — You understand the industry and pain points
5. **Repeat buyers** — Businesses that need leads continuously

### Lead Collection Workflow

1. **Define ICP** (Ideal Customer Profile) — Industry, size, location, title
2. **Build search query** — Use your data source to find matching contacts
3. **Verify data** — Check email validity, phone accuracy
4. **Enrich leads** — Add company info, social profiles, intent data
5. **Organize in CRM** — Spreadsheet or CRM tool
6. **Deliver to client** — CSV, Google Sheet, or CRM integration

## Step 4: Find Clients to Buy Your Leads

### Client Acquisition

**LinkedIn Outreach:**
```
Hi [Name],

I help [industry] companies fill their pipeline with qualified
leads. I've built a system that identifies [target persona]
who are actively looking for [service].

I'd love to send you 5 free sample leads so you can see the
quality. No strings attached.

Would that be useful?

Best,
[Your name]
```

**Cold Email:**
- Target: Founders, sales managers, marketing directors
- Offer: Free sample leads → paid subscription
- Follow up: 3-email sequence over 7 days

## Step 5: Deliver & Scale

### Delivery Standards

- [ ] All emails verified (bounce rate < 3%)
- [ ] Complete data fields (name, email, phone, company, title)
- [ ] Organized in spreadsheet with clear columns
- [ ] No duplicate leads
- [ ] Fresh leads (collected within past 30 days)
- [ ] Replacement policy for invalid leads

### Scaling Strategies

1. **Automate collection** — Use scraping tools and APIs
2. **Hire VAs** — Data entry and verification ($3–$5/hour)
3. **Add qualification** — Call leads to verify interest (2–5x price increase)
4. **Build a marketplace** — Connect lead buyers and sellers
5. **Create niche reports** — Industry-specific lead reports ($100–$500 each)

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

1. **Selling unverified leads** — High bounce rate = lost clients
2. **No niche focus** — General leads are worth 10x less than niche leads
3. **Ignoring data privacy** — GDPR, CAN-SPAM, and local laws matter
4. **One-time sales** — Subscriptions are worth 5–10x more than one-off sales
5. **Not replacing bad leads** — One bad batch can lose a client forever
