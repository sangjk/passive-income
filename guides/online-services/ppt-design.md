# 🎨 PPT Design — Side Hustle Guide

Create professional presentations for clients using templates. List your services on freelance platforms and earn $50–$500 per project.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Difficulty** | ⭐⭐ (Easy–Medium) |
| **Time to First $** | 1–3 days |

| **Upfront Investment** | $0–$30 (template packs) |

## Step 2: Build Your Portfolio

Before getting clients, you need proof of your work.

### Portfolio Strategy

1. **Create 5–10 sample presentations** across different niches:
   - Business pitch deck
   - Marketing strategy presentation
   - Educational/course slides
   - Startup investor deck
   - Company report / quarterly review

2. **Show before/after transformations** — Take a boring slide and redesign it

3. **Host your portfolio on:**
   - Behance (free, professional)
   - your own website (Carrd.co — $19/year)
   - Google Drive folder (free, easy to share)

| **Upwork** | Longer projects, higher pay | $50–$200 |

| **Local businesses** | Recurring work | $150–$500 |

### Fiverr Gig Setup

**Title:** "I will design professional PowerPoint presentations"

**Pricing Tiers:**
- **Basic ($25):** Up to 10 slides, 2 revisions, 3-day delivery
- **Standard ($50):** Up to 20 slides, 3 revisions, 2-day delivery
- **Premium ($100):** Up to 40 slides, unlimited revisions, 1-day delivery

### Cold Outreach Script

```
Hi [Name],

I noticed [Company] recently presented at [Event]/posted about [Topic].
I specialize in transforming presentations from "meh" to memorable.

I'd love to redesign 2-3 slides for you for FREE so you can see the
difference. No strings attached.

Would that be helpful?

Best,
[Your name]
```

## Step 4: Deliver & Scale

### Delivery Checklist

- [ ] Consistent color scheme throughout
- [ ] Readable fonts (min 24pt for body, 36pt for headers)
- [ ] High-resolution images (no pixelation)
- [ ] Aligned elements (use grid/guides)
- [ ] Spell-check complete
- [ ] Export in both PPTX and PDF formats

### Scaling Strategies

1. **Create template packs** — Sell on Creative Market, Etsy ($10–$30 each)
2. **Offer subscription services** — Monthly retainer for regular clients ($200–$500/mo)
3. **Hire junior designers** — Outsource simpler work, keep 40–60% margin
4. **Upsell related services** — Infographics, social media graphics, brand guidelines

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

1. **Overdesigning** — Clients want clear, professional slides, not art projects
2. **Ignoring brand guidelines** — Always ask for brand colors/fonts first
3. **Too much text per slide** — Maximum 6 bullet points or 30 words per slide
4. **Not setting revision limits** — Scope creep kills profitability
5. **Pricing too low** — Don't race to the bottom; compete on quality
