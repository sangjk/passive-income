## Step 1: Learn the Craft

### Resume Writing Fundamentals

| Principle | Why It Matters |
|-----------|---------------|
| **ATS Optimization** | 75% of resumes are rejected by Applicant Tracking Systems before a human sees them |
| **Quantified Achievements** | "Increased sales by 30%" beats "Responsible for sales" |
| **Targeted Content** | Each resume should be tailored to the specific job posting |
| **Clean Formatting** | Simple, scannable layout with clear sections |
| **Action Verbs** | Start every bullet with a strong action verb |

### ATS-Friendly Rules

1. Use standard section headers: Experience, Education, Skills
2. Avoid tables, columns, graphics, and text boxes
3. Use standard fonts: Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman
4. Save as .docx (most ATS-friendly format)
5. Include keywords from the job description

### Free Learning Resources

- **Resume Worded** — AI-powered resume scoring
- **Jobscan** — ATS optimization tool
- **r/resumes** — Community feedback on Reddit
- **The Ladders** — Resume studies and best practices

## Step 5: Deliver & Scale

### Workflow

1. **Intake form** — Collect current resume, target job description, career goals
2. **Initial review** — Identify top 5 issues (30 min)
3. **First draft** — Complete rewrite (1–2 hours)
4. **Client review** — One round of revisions (30 min)
5. **Final delivery** — DOCX + PDF versions

### Scaling Strategies

1. **Create resume templates** — Sell on Etsy/Creative Market ($5–$15 each)
2. **Offer group workshops** — Teach resume writing to job search groups ($30–$50/person)
3. **Build a course** — "Write Your Own Winning Resume" ($49–$99)
4. **Hire writers** — Train junior writers, keep 40–50% margin
5. **Add LinkedIn optimization** — Upsell existing clients ($50–$100 extra)

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

1. **Ignoring ATS** — A beautiful resume that gets filtered out is useless
2. **Making claims you can't verify** — Don't guarantee job placement
3. **Overusing buzzwords** — "Synergy," "go-getter," "ninja" are red flags
4. **One-size-fits-all** — Each resume must be targeted to the specific role
5. **No intake process** — You need client info to write effectively
