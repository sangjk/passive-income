# 📋 Side Hustle Starter Templates

Ready-to-use templates for launching your side hustle. Copy and customize for your needs.

---

## 1. Freelance Service Agreement

```
FREELANCE SERVICE AGREEMENT

Between:
  Client: [Full Name / Company Name]
  Freelancer: [Your Name]

PROJECT DETAILS:
  Service: [Description of service]
  Deliverables: [List of deliverables]
  Timeline: [Start date] to [End date]

PAYMENT:
  Total Fee: $[Amount]
  Payment Schedule:
    - 50% upfront: $[Amount] due on [Date]
    - 50% on delivery: $[Amount] due on [Date]
  Payment Method: [PayPal / Stripe / Bank Transfer]

REVISIONS:
  Included revisions: [Number] rounds
  Additional revisions: $[Amount] per round

TERMS:
  - Client provides all necessary materials by [Date]
  - Freelancer delivers work by [Date]
  - Late payment incurs [X]% fee after [Days] days
  - Either party may terminate with [Days] notice
  - Intellectual property transfers upon final payment

SIGNATURES:
  Client: _____________________ Date: _________
  Freelancer: _________________ Date: _________
```

## 7. Cold Outreach Email Templates

### Template A: Free Sample Offer
```
Subject: Free [service] sample for [Company]

Hi [Name],

I noticed [Company] [observation about their business].
I specialize in [your service] and I'd love to offer
a free [sample/demo] so you can see the quality firsthand.

No strings attached — just want to show you what I can do.

Would that be helpful?

Best,
[Your name]
```

### Template B: Problem-Solution
```
Subject: Solving [specific problem] at [Company]

Hi [Name],

I help businesses like [Company] solve [specific problem].
My clients typically see [result] within [timeframe].

I put together a quick analysis of how this could work
for [Company]. Mind if I send it over?

Best,
[Your name]
```

### Template C: Social Proof
```
Subject: How [similar company] achieved [result]

Hi [Name],

I recently helped [similar company] achieve [specific result]
through [your service]. Given that [Company] is in a similar
space, I thought you might be interested.

I'd love to chat for 10 minutes about how we could
replicate these results for you. Available this week?

Best,
[Your name]
```
